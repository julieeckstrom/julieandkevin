Location & Directions

The weekend will take place at Gateway Lodge in Cook Forest. We first discovered the old lodge when sneaking away from Pittsburgh to spend a weekend glamping, and we can’t wait to share the lodge and Cook Forest State Park with all of you!

Gateway Lodge
14870 PA-36
Cooksburg, PA 16217

Gateway Lodge is about a 2 hour drive north of Pittsburgh. Since many may be flying in, you can look for flights to the Pittsburgh International Airport, or to the Cleveland Hopkins International airport if that’s more convenient, which is about 2 hours and 30 minutes from Gateway.

Directions

Since so many will be driving from different origins, we decided to not write out directions from one location. Instead, use this Google Maps link to ensure you're going to the right location.

Lodging

Some are staying at Gateway Lodge

Our closest friends and family have been invited to stay with us at the lodge, which unfortunately has too few rooms for all the guests we want to invite!

Campsites

If you’d like to be even closer to nature, there are plenty of campgrounds nearby. The state park campground is just across the river from the lodge. A few guests have also decided to stay at Camper's Paradise, about a 15 minute drive from the lodge.

Hotels 20 minutes away in Clarion, PA

The nearest town to Gateway Lodge is Clarion, Pennsylvania, about a 20 minute drive to the lodge. There are a few hotels here, including the Holiday Inn Express & Suites Clarion and Hampton Inn Clarion.

Good to Know

Cook Forest is beautiful with a lot to do. There's river tubing, kayaking, and conoeing, hiking, trail riding, and more. You can find out more about the area on the state park website.

Please keep in mind when choosing shoes that the ceremony and cocktail hour will be in a grassy yard.

The weather in June is typically a high in the high 70s and low in the mid 50s. If it's a typical day, it'll feel warm.

About Gifts

When thinking about what we wanted to do for our wedding, we quickly realized that we wanted to deemphasize all of the pomp around a wedding and just spend time with our family and friends. We truly, honestly feel that the best gift anyone could give to us is their presence during the weekend. We know it will be a trek for some to join us, and because of this we would much rather enjoy time with you than receive a gift.

Still, some people have asked us what we would enjoy. We don’t want to make this harder for people, so we put together a small registry on Amazon and set up this newlywed fund on The Knot should you somehow feel that your presence at Gateway and Cook Forest isn’t enough.
